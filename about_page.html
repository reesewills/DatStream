About


The Brand


DatStream makes use of cutting edge technology to bring together music from many different streaming services.

The Mission


Our mission is to revolutionize fluent listening. We have found that music has become very competetive and exclusive. We hope to bring as much music together as possible to make listening to music about the experience, not about which steaming service to use.


Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start making my collection?

    Start by creating your account! From there, all you need to do is go to search music and find some songs that you like. You can easily add them to playlists from there and begin collecting your music!

  • How do I create my account?

    Simply sign up by going clicking sign up in the top right hand corner.

  • Can I only use 2 of the 3 streaming services if I do not like one of them?

    Yes! DatStream does not force you to utilize all of the streaming services we offer, we simply provide a few to choose from so that you can have access to the most music possible.

  • What streaming services are currently offered with DatStream?

    DatStream currently works with Soundcloud, Spotify, and Youtube.

  • Is DatStream working to add more services to stream?

    Yes! We are always working to improve our app.

  • Can I save playlists for later?

    Yes, DatStream allows users who have an account to save playlists to listen to later.
